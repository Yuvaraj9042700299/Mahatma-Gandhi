Mahatma Gandhi

Mahatma Gandhi was a prominent Indian leader who played a key role in the Indian independence movement through non-violent civil disobedience. He was born on October 2, 1869, in Porbandar, India.

Gandhi's leadership and principles of truth, non-violence, and peaceful resistance inspired people all over the world. His famous quote, "Be the change that you wish to see in the world," continues to influence generations.
